About the Department

Learn about our mission, people, history, and the facilities that power innovation at the Department of CSE, University of Barishal.

Who We Are

We combine strong theoretical foundations with hands-on practice. Our courses, labs, and mentorship help students become leaders in academia, industry, and entrepreneurship.

  • Outcome-based curriculum
  • Research-driven culture
  • Industry partnerships
  • Student clubs & events

Our academic environment is inclusive and collaborative, with opportunities for undergraduate research, hackathons, and internships.

Our Mission

To educate and inspire students to become creative, ethical, and skilled computer engineers capable of advancing technology for social good.

  • High-quality teaching & mentoring
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Real-world, impact-oriented projects

Our Vision

To be a nationally recognized center of excellence in computer science education and research, fostering innovation and entrepreneurship.

  • Interdisciplinary innovation
  • Sustainable development
  • Global collaborations
