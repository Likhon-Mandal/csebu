Eligibility (B.Sc.)

  • Completed HSC/A-Level or equivalent with Math & Physics
  • Minimum GPA/grades per university policy
  • Entrance test/interview as applicable

Eligibility (M.Sc.)

  • B.Sc. in CSE/related field
  • Minimum CGPA requirement
  • Statement of purpose & references

Required Documents

  • Academic transcripts & certificates
  • National ID/Passport photo page
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • English proficiency (if required)
